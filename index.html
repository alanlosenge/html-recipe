Ugali Nyama

  • Ingredients
  • Instructions
  • Tips

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of maize meal (cornmeal)
  • 4 cups of water
  • A pinch of salt (optional)
  • 1 pound of beef, cubed
  • 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 tomatoes, diced
  • 1 green bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 cup of beef broth
  • 1 teaspoon of paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon of cayenne pepper (adjust to your spice preference)
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley or cilantro for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. Place the 2 cups of maize meal (cornmeal) in a large mixing bowl.
  2. In a separate pot, bring 4 cups of water to a boil. If desired, add a pinch of salt for flavor.
  3. Slowly pour the hot water over the maize meal while stirring continuously to prevent lumps. Continue to stir until the mixture thickens and pulls away from the sides of the pot. This is your Ugali. Remove it from heat.
  4. In a large skillet, heat 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil over medium heat.
  5. Add the finely chopped onion and minced garlic to the skillet. Saut√© until the onion becomes translucent.
  6. Add the cubed beef to the skillet and cook until browned.
  7. Stir in the diced tomatoes and chopped green bell pepper. Cook until the vegetables soften.
  8. Pour in the beef broth and add paprika, cayenne pepper, salt, and black pepper. Simmer for 15-20 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld and the sauce to thicken.
  9. Serve the Ugali alongside the beef stew, garnishing with fresh parsley or cilantro if desired.

Enjoy!!!!!
